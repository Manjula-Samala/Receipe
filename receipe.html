Chicken Fry

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (with bone or boneless)
  • 1 cup curd (yogurt)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 cups basmati rice (soaked 20-30 min)
  • 2-3 tbsp oil or ghee

Preparation Steps

  1. Marinate chicken in curd, chili powder, turmeric, salt, and ginger-garlic paste (20 mins).
  2. Fry onions in a pan until golden, remove half for later.
  3. Cook marinated chicken with remaining onions until tender.
  4. Cook rice separately (70% done), drain.
  5. Layer rice over chicken in the pan, top with saved onions.
  6. Cover tightly and cook on low for 10–15 mins (or until rice is fully done).
